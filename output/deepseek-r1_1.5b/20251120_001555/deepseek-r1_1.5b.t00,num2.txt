To calculate \( 15 \times 24 \), follow these simple steps:

1. **Break down the numbers based on place value:**
   - \( 15 = 10 + 5 \)
   - \( 24 = 20 + 4 \)

2. **Multiply each part separately:**
   
   - \( (10) \times (20) = 200 \)
   - \( (10) \times (4) = 40 \)
   - \( (5) \times (20) = 100 \)
   - \( (5) \times (4) = 20 \)

3. **Add all the products together:**
   
   \[
   200 + 40 + 100 + 20 = 360
   \]

**Final Answer:**  
\[
\boxed{360}
\]
