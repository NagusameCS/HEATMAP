To calculate \(15 \times 24\), follow these steps:

### Step-by-Step Solution:

1. **Break Down the Numbers:**
   - Break down 24 into simpler components:
     \[
     24 = 20 + 4
     \]
   
2. **Multiply by Each Component Separately:**
   - Multiply \(15\) by \(20\):
     \[
     15 \times 20 = 300
     \]
   - Multiply \(15\) by \(4\):
     \[
     15 \times 4 = 60
     \]

3. **Add the Results Together:**
   \[
   300 + 60 = 360
   \]

### Final Answer:
\[
\boxed{360}
\]
