To calculate \(15 \times 24\), follow these simple steps:

1. **Break down the numbers**:
   - Multiply \(10\) by \(24\):
     \[
     10 \times 24 = 240
     \]
   
2. **Multiply the remaining part**:
   - Multiply \(5\) by \(24\):
     \[
     5 \times 24 = 120
     \]

3. **Add the results together**:
   \[
   240 + 120 = 360
   \]

Therefore, \(15 \times 24 = \boxed{360}\).
