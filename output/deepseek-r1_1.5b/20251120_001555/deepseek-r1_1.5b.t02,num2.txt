To calculate \( 15 \times 24 \), follow these easy steps:

1. **Break down the numbers:**
   
   - \( 24 = 20 + 4 \)

2. **Multiply by each part separately:**

   - \( 15 \times 20 = 300 \)
   - \( 15 \times 4 = 60 \)

3. **Add the results together:**
   
   \[
   300 + 60 = 360
   \]

**Final Answer:**

\[
\boxed{360}
\]
