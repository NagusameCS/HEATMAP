To calculate \(15 \times 24\), follow these steps:

1. **Break down the multiplication:**
   
   \[
   15 \times 24 = (10 + 5) \times 24
   \]

2. **Distribute the multiplication across the addition:**

   \[
   = 10 \times 24 + 5 \times 24
   \]

3. **Calculate each part separately:**
   
   - \(10 \times 24 = 240\)
   - \(5 \times 24 = 120\)

4. **Add the results together:**
   
   \[
   240 + 120 = 360
   \]

**Final Answer:** \(\boxed{360}\)
