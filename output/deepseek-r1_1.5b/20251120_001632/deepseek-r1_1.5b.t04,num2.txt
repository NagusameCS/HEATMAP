To calculate \( 15 \times 24 \), follow these steps:

1. **Breakdown the Numbers**:
   - \( 15 \) can be expressed as \( 10 + 5 \).
   - \( 24 \) is already a two-digit number: \( 2 \times 10 + 4 \).

2. **Multiply Each Part**:
   
   - Multiply \( 10 \) by \( 24 \):
     \[
     10 \times 24 = 240
     \]
     
   - Multiply \( 5 \) by \( 24 \):
     \[
     5 \times 24 = 120
     \]

3. **Add the Results Together**:
   \[
   240 + 120 = 360
   \]

\(\boxed{360}\)
